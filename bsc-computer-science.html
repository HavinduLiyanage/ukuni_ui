Programme Overview

The BSc Computer Science degree at Coventry University is designed to produce graduates with the technical, professional, and entrepreneurial skills required to excel in the rapidly evolving tech industry. You will explore fundamental concepts of computing, software engineering, and artificial intelligence.

Our activity-led learning approach means you will be engaged in practical, hands-on projects from day one. You'll have access to our modern computing labs, equipped with industry-standard software and hardware, ensuring you are well-prepared for real-world challenges.

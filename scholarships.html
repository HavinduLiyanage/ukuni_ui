Am I Eligible?

Most scholarships are open to Sri Lankan students who apply through UKUniAdmissions. Eligibility varies by university and award type, but common criteria include:

  • check_circle

    Strong Academic Results

    A/L or equivalent qualifications meeting the university's entry requirements

  • check_circle

    IELTS / English Proficiency

    IELTS score meeting or exceeding the programme requirement (typically 6.0–6.5)

  • check_circle

    Applying for September / January Intake

    Applications submitted before the scholarship deadline for the relevant intake

  • check_circle

    Applying Through UKUniAdmissions

    Our partner university scholarships are available exclusively to students applying via our network

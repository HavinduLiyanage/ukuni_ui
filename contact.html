Frequently Asked Questions

Is the initial consultation really free?

expand_more
Yes, your first consultation is completely free of charge. We use this time to understand your academic background, goals, and provide a high-level overview of your options without any obligation.

What should I prepare for the call?

expand_more
It's helpful to have your academic transcripts, any English proficiency test scores (like IELTS), and a general idea of the subjects you wish to study. Don't worry if you don't have everything yet!

How long does a typical consultation last?

expand_more
Initial consultations usually take about 30 to 45 minutes, giving us ample time to discuss your profile and map out potential next steps.
